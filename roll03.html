🎞 EP03 — Hailey in Hong Kong

Kodak Gold 200 · Dec 2025 · Hailey's visit

This roll records the visit of my friend Hailey.
We were university classmates, but now we live far apart and rarely get the chance to meet. She came to Hong Kong for a short visit, and we spent three wonderful days together. We explored the city, tried lots of food, and even visited Disneyland. Because moments like this are rare, I wanted to capture as many memories as possible on film.
